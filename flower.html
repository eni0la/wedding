MIA MINI

You asked, we listened… Introducing MIA Mini. These styles have been sustainably designed and each piece is 100% produced from the material offcuts of our bridal gowns. Designed with the little adventurer in mind, Mini MIA can run, skip, play and explore in unrestricted comfort. There are no zips or buttons in these outfits — they are stretch fits, so they will grow with our girls. These beautiful creations are for the women of tomorrow, the little firecrackers who go against the norm, the non-pink wearing renegades, the girls who ask more questions, read more books, and be the first one down the big scary slide. Rebellious girl, this one’s for you.
