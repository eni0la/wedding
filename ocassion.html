We don’t believe a bridesmaid or occasion dress should be worn once and never seen again! Instead, we wholeheartedly believe that our clothes should be loved and worn for a lifetime. We have come to understand that the days of traditional bridesmaids are fast fading. For you, it’s more about creating a capsule collection for your bridesmaids. You can do this by selecting an edit of dresses as individual and unique as the girls themselves. We designed each piece with the intention to celebrate every woman and her perfect female form.

In that case, your wedding day will be remembered for all the right reasons.
